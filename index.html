Your record of every appointment

Every professional has a record of that meeting. Now you do too.

MyScribe gives you your own plain-language record of every professional encounter. On your phone. Before you reach the door.

Free forever. The app is free to download and free to use. The employer of the professional carries the cost. You are not the product.

How it works

One sentence. Then everything is automatic.

1

Open the app

No login. No setup. Straight to the record screen.

2

Ask if you can record

"Do you mind if I record this, just for my own records?" That is the only thing you need to say or do.

3

Press record. Have your appointment.

One button. Be present in the conversation.

4

Press stop. Your After Note arrives.

Plain language. On your phone before you reach the door. MyScribe can read it to you aloud, in any language, in the waiting room, on the bus, or when you get home. Everything happens on your phone. No signal needed.

What an After Note contains

The same four things. Every appointment. Every setting.

GP or social worker. Housing officer or solicitor. The structure is always the same. The content is always specific to the person and the moment.

MyScribe  ·  After Note  ·  anatomy
Your appointment on [date] and what it means for you
What was agreed
What the professional said. What changed. What was decided. In plain language, without clinical shorthand.
What comes next
What you need to do. When. In what order. The action, not the advice.
What to watch for
When to act. When to wait. Who to contact if things change before the next appointment.
How to get more help
One link. Neighbourhood-specific. The one piece of local information this person needs most.

Questions

The ones people ask.

"Who pays if it's free to me?"
The employer of the professional pays, never the citizen. The GP practice. The local authority. The law firm. The housing association. Whoever employs the person sitting across the desk pays a penny every time that appointment generates an After Note. The patient receives it free. Always.
"Can I use it for any appointment?"
Any professional encounter where a record matters. GP, social worker, mental health worker, housing officer, solicitor, probation officer, benefits assessor. Any conversation where the other person leaves with notes and you leave with memory.
"What does it do with my data?"
Your After Note lives on your device. No archive. No account means no data held centrally. When MyScribe reads your After Note to you, that happens on your phone too. No third party server. No signal required. Even the voice that reads it to you never leaves your phone.
